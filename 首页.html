Tsinghua University

Founded in 1911, it is a famous institution of higher learning in China, located in the scenic Tsinghua Garden in the northwest suburbs of Beijing。

Jingshan Park

Jingshan Park is located at the center point of the north-south central axis of Beijing, surrounded by the Forbidden City in the south, Beihai in the west, and Drum Tower in the north, which was once the highest point in the center of Beijing.

Lama temple

Located in the northeast corner of the Dongcheng District of Beijing, that is, east of Lama Temple Street, it is the largest Tibetan Buddhist monastery in Beijing。
